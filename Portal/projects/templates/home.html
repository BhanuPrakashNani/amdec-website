About ASoC

Amrita Season of Code is a 5-week long online programme conducted by FOSS@Amrita, for the students of Amrita University, who are new to the world of open source software development. The participants through the programme will contribute to the projects of ASoC mentors, and will be guided by them. The programme not only helps students to start contributing to open source, but also prepare them for Google Sumer of Code.
